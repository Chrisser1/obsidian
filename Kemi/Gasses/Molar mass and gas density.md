Rearranging the ideal gas law from [[Ideal gases]] we get:
$$
\frac{V}{n} = \frac{RT}{P}
$$
Where $\frac{V}{n}$ is called the molar volume. 

## Gas density
$$
\text{gas density} (g/L) = \frac{\text{molar mass}}{\text{molar volume}}
$$
